---
title: Show (actor, flow property) 
description: Definition & use of Show (actor, flow property) 
---
In a Measure IQ actor or flow property, **Show** is the [method](https://behavure.ai/docs/wiki/spaces/SGV/pages/2160231530/Method+v5) used to set what you want to measure in a query. Examples include count events, count users, or max or min of an attribute.
