---
title: Show (actor, flow property)
description: Definition & use of Show (actor, flow property)
---

In a Measure IQ actor or flow property, **Show** is the [method](/measure_iq/glossary/method) used to set what you want to measure in a query. Examples include count events, count users, or max or min of an attribute.
